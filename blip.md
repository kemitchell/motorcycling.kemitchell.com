---
title: Blipping
---

## Technique

### Front Brake
- Maintain consistent pressure.
- Use index and middle fingers.

### Throttle
- Use ring and pinky fingers.

### Clutch
- Pull in fast.
- Let out more gradually.

### Transmission
- Shift down.

## Drilling
- Shift all the way up, then blip all the way down.
